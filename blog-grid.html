Bahria Town receives an award
News

18 Sep. 2021

Bahria Town received an award at the Business Forum & Conferences – “The Corridors of Connectivity” (Pakistan and Kyrgyzstan in the Age of Socio-Economic Integration). The conference was held on 27-28 October 2021 in Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan. Mr. Sajid Ilyas (Deputy Chief Executive Bahria Town) received the award on behalf of Bahria Town & representing business community from Pakistan. This two-day bilateral Pakistan-Kyrgyzstan business forum and conference aim at exploring bilateral relations between Pakistan and Kyrgyzstan on Health, Business & Education.

Bahria Town International Hospital
News

18 Dec. 2021

Karachi: Bahria Town International Hospital Karachi organized a free heart screening clinic to commemorate the World Heart Day. Free consultation, blood sugar testing, ECG, BMI, blood pressure and pulse were provided under the supervision of renowned Consultant Interventional Cardiologist Dr. Yasir Paracha and Consultant General Physician Dr. Humayun Bin Irfan. Prominent TV and Film personality Mr. Yasir Hussain was also present to support the cause. He also expressed his views on heart day and told the audience about how impressed he is with the Hospital’s services, staff and cleanliness. Our Interventional Cardiologist Dr. Yasir Paracha and General Physician Dr. Humayun bin Irfan examined the patients here. All the patients were happy and they also thanked the hospital and Bahria Town management. The activity was also part of the overall mission to inculcate awareness among the masses about the importance of regular medical checkups and early diagnosis. Professional and highly qualified doctors, trained nursing and paramedic staff, availability of top-notch machinery and top of the line medical facilities has caused positive word of mouth which consequently attracted ever-increasing footfall. Patients are not only coming from Karachi but from interior Sindh and Baluchistan. .

Six complex surgeries were successfully performed
News

12 Dec. 2021

Lahore: Six complex surgeries have been successfully performed at Bahria Town International Hospital and Pakistan Children’s Heart Foundation after conducting Pediatric Cardiac Surgical Mission. These surgeries were performed on children aged 6 months to 10 years. Renowned cardiac surgeons from Medina and Saudi Arabia, Dr. Aitzaz-ud-Din Syed, Team Anaesthesia and ICU Care Team, Major Gen (R) Iftikhar and Dr. Asif Akbar (Peads Cardiologist) performed the surgeries together. This mission was started from 11th to 16th July, 2021. Dr. Aitzaz-ud-Din Syed is the heart of very experienced children’s who have been serving in UK, USA and Madinah for a long time. Bahria Town International Hospital has been chosen because of its state-of-the-art facilities. People had to go out for this complex operation but now these operations will be performed at Bahria Town Hospital in Pakistan as well. This facility is now available in Pakistan at a very low cost and deserving people can take full advantage of it. During this mission, we will perform surgeries on as many deserving and congenital heart disease patients as possible. All the departments at Bahria Town One International Hospital Orchard are equipped with state-of-the-art facilities with the best machinery and equipment in the world. Successful Liver and Kidney Transplants for Children and Adults at Bahria International Hospital Orchard, there is an ICU with access to international facilities where the training staff is performing their duties. At Bahria Town Hospital, Richard, patients not only come from Pakistan but also from abroad and benefit from international facilities. .

new

28 Sep. 2021

Lahore: MOU signing between Bahria Town International Hospital and Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS) for Understanding to invest in the development of health care managers and professionals through The MS Healthcare Management and Innovation (HMI) Programs. The signing ceremony was attended by the staff of Bahria International Hospital, Shifa International Hospital, Shaukat Khanum and LUMS. Ramadan Ration Campaign 2021
new

4 May at 11:01 AM

Karachi: Bahria Town once again geared up to provide ease and support to people during this Ramadan through its Ramadan Asaan Campaign. Under the banner of this campaign, Bahria Town Karachi management successfully distributed ration among more than 3500 households in 72 different villages scattered around M9 motorway and Bahria Town. Under the guidance of Senior General Manager Capt. (R) Hamid Zafar, teams successfully distributed ration in the areas of Deh Langeji, Deh Konkar, Deh Bolehri, Deh Mole, Dehsana Channa, Deh Palari etc. where more than 20,000 people were reached. The ration parcel contained 20 kg flour, 10 kg rice, Oil, tea, sugar, sorbet etc. In the next phase of the campaign, ration will be provided to 12,000 underprivileged employees of Bahria Town Islamabad, Lahore and Karachi where 6000 parcels are allocated for deserving employees in Karachi. Bahria Town continue to provide free meals (aftaari) to thousands of people daily during the month of Ramadan from 11 of its Dastarkhwan outlets.

BAHRIA TOWN HOSPITAL LAHORE CONDUCTS FREE MEDICAL TESTS
new

11 Mar at 5:14 PM

LAHORE: Bahria Town Hospital Lahore, being part of the 3rd Health Expo, conducted a medical symposium, free consultancy camp & free medical tests for the general public at Expo centre. Thousands of people benefited from free Hepatitis B & C screening, blood sugar screening, body mass index, BP, blood grouping, vision & hearing test as well as free medical checkup & consultancy. Towards the end of the Expo a lucky draw for 2 free hair transplants was carried out and the winners were announced. Brig (R) Dr. Ghulam Mehdi, Prof. Dr. Jawad Sajid Khan, Dr. Nauman Naseer, Dr. Amer Khan, Dr. Qasim Bashir, Prof. Dr. Imtiaz Rasool, Dr. Iftikhar Ahmed Bhatti, Dr. Raja Yasir Shahbaz, Dr. Khawar Nazir & Dr. Atif Ikram also gave lectures on important medical issues for public awareness in the medical symposium.
